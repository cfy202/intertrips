Day 03

Beijing

Hutong tour with rickshaw & lunch at local family

Beijing Hutongs are made up of lanes or alleys formed by lines of pre-modern quadrangles (siheyuan), four-sided dwelling compounds or quadrangles consist of one-story courtyard houses, which make up the old Beijing, and featured typical Chinese residential architecture. Hutongs are an irregular maze, ranging from only 40 centimeter to 10 meter in width, and the longest one has more than 20 turns. Exploring Hutongs is a challenge to you if you do not know the landform. You can hop on a rickshaw to ride through the narrow alleys. A local Hutong family visit will be specially arranged for you. You can talk with the local residents and have lunch there. If you would like to have a glimpse into Beijing's yesterday, you are highly recommended to have a short adventure trip to the Hutongs.

$39
Day 04

Beijing

The Golden Mask Dynasty

The Golden Mask Dynasty is a large-scale Chinese musical drama, performed at the OCT Theater in Beijing Happy Valley. The performance is fairy-tale like story of war, royal banquets, and romance. With the Ancient Sanxingdui Civilization as times background, it tells the story of the beautiful Golden Mask Queen who built a glorious kingdom with her wisdom, tolerance, trust and love. The performance has eight chapters and included more than 200 actors from China and abroad. Produced by Chinese playwrights, directors and designers, the play features Chinese dances, acrobatics, costumes, and lighting and acoustics. Coming here, you will be fully immersed in the ancient romantic legend and the marvelous Chinese art.

$49
Day 05

Suzhou

Boat ride on the Grand Canal with walk around the local market

Known as the "Venice of the East", the classical gardens in Suzhou were added to the list of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites in 1997 and 2000. You will have a boat trip on the world-famous Beijing-Hangzhou Grand Canal, which is the earliest and longest manmade watercourse in the world. In the long history, it   has made enormous contribution to the exchance between northern China and southern China in both economy and culture.

$29
Day 07

Shanghai

Shanghai Lujiazui FTDZ & Huang Pu River Cruise

Located on the east bank of the Huangpu River in Pudong, across from the Bund, Lujiazui Finance and Trade Zone is the key financial center in Shanghai. It is surrounded by the Oriental Pearl Tower, Jin Mao Tower, Shanghai Tower, Shanghai World Financial Center and a large number of business buildings. Huangpu River is the city's shipping artery both to the East China Sea and the mouth of the Yangtze River. Huangpu River Cruise is just one of the best ways to have a look both old and new Shanghai. You'll see the magnificent building skylines on both the historic west side (the Bund), and the modern east side (Pudong). A spectacular view is that the two suspension bridges, Nanpu Bridge and Yangpu Bridge.

$45
Day 08

Shanghai

Full Day Yu Yuan Garden & Jade Buddha with lunch

Your tour today starts with Jade Buddha Temple, built during the Qing Dynasty. It is famous for two beautiful white-jade Buddhas brought from Myanmar, one recumbent on a redwood bed, the other seated on a lotus flower. Then drive to pay a visit to Nanjing Road. After enjoying the lunch, head to Yu Garden, the most beautiful garden south of the Yangtze River. The garden is built about 400 years ago during the Ming Dynasty by Pan Yunduan, a high-ranking government official. The total area covers 20,000 square meters, and features pavilions, ponds, rockeries, cloisters etc. Afterwards, have some free time at the local bazaar of Shanghai Old Street ( City God Temple) area.

$55

Terra Cotta Army 1 Day Package with Air from Shanghai

Fly to historic Xi'an in early morning, which was the ancient capital of China and site of archaeological treasures. 

Upon arrival, our guide will take you to modern China's greatest archaeological discovery - The Terra Cotta Warriors Museum, which silently guarded the tomb of China's First Emperor for over 2,200 years. Next you will head to the Terra Cotta Replication Factory that actually re-made terra cotta soldiers. Visit Bell Tower later before head to airport. 

Fly back to Shanghai in late evening, Tour Guide in Shanghai will pick you up and transfer back to Hotel.

$599

Permanent Show "ERA - Intersection of Time" -

The show "ERA" in Shanghai has become one of the most popular evening entertainments for tourists in the city, which integrating traditional love story with modern performing art. ERA consists of many exciting elements, including acrobatics, dances, dramas, music, martial arts, etc. Not only amazed by the acrobats' control and precision, but also you will be enchanted by the world that is created through the use of multimedia, technology, lighting and sound effects, elaborate costumes, original live music and a lot more. ERA can remain universal, without language or cultural barriers. A thousand-year-old gesture is worth a thousand emotions, a thousand images, a thousand words... No wonder people say: "Miss it and you miss Shanghai".

$55
Day 09

Shanghai

Mt Huangshan 4 Days Package with Bullet Train from Shanghai

Day 1, Shanghai - Mt. Huangshan

Head to Mt Huangshan with Bullet Train (2nd class soft seat, 4 hours), Tour guide will greet and transfer you to hotel.

Enjoy the rest on your own, or join half day tour to Ancient Village Xidi at $59 per person, which was declared a part of the "Ancient Villages in Southern Anhui" World Heritage Site by UNESCO in 2000.

Hotel: Huangshan International Hotel or Similar

Day 2, Mt. Huangshan (American Breakfast, Lunch) 

Mountain Huangshan, known as 'the loveliest mountain of China', was acclaimed through art and literature during a good part of Chinese history (e.g. the Shanshui 'mountain and water' style of the mid-16th century). Today it holds the same fascination for visitors, poets, painters and photographers who come on pilgrimage to the site, which is renowned for its magnificent scenery made up of many granite peaks and rocks emerging out of a sea of clouds.

Today you’ll spend a full day at the Mt. Huangshan. The round trip cable car is included. During the 10 minutes cable car journey, you’ll enjoy the magnificent scenery. Tour guide will accompany you to visit the highlights of Mt. Huangshan, such as North Sea and West Sea area, where you can appreciate the breathtaking scenery and photograph the seas of clouds, peculiar-shaped rocks, verdant trees, and rolling mountains.Then we visit the Tunxi Old Street, an ancient commercial street in center of Tunxi Town.

Hotel: Huangshan International Hotel or Similar

Day 3, Mt Huangshan - Shanghai (American Breakfast) 

Spend more time in Mt Huangshan Area, then stop at Bamboo Fiber Exhibition Center before heading to raiway station to board Bullet Train (2nd class soft seat, 13:50 PM - 17:55 PM).

Bus driver in Shanghai will pick you up and drive you back to hotel.

Hotel: Renaissance Shanghai Caohejing Hotel or Similar

Day 4, Depart from Shanghai (American Breakfast)

Airport drop off, home your sweet home.

Hong Kong 3 Days Package with Air from Shanghai

Day 1, Fly to Hong Kong from Shanghai

Please take hotel shuttle bus back to your hotel upon arrival. 

Day 2, Hong Kong

Half day sightseeing coach tour to Victoria Peak for panoramic views of Hong Kong Island, Kowloon and the surrounding islands. You will pass picturesque Repulse Bay on the way to Fishing Village of Aberdeen. Here you'll see Hong Kong's junk-dwelling floating community still very much a part of Hong Kong life. You'll have the opportunity to board a sampan, for a close-up view of waterborne life. Followed with a visit to Stanley Market, where you can pick up bargains at just a fraction of the price in town. Stop at Prestigious Jewelry before head back to your hotel.

Day 3, Depart from Hong Kong

Take hotel shuttle bus back to airport for your home.
