Groupon Voucher Redemption

1. Redemption occurs upon booking. Groupon voucher value is only valid for bookings made before the expiration date.
2. Groupon voucher value is non-transferrable and non-refundable after redemption. Please do purchase Travel Insurance to protect your own benefits.
3. Each traveler needs one voucher.
4. Single Supplement Fee applies if there is only one traveler in the booking.
5. Optional Tours, Travel Insurance, and China Visa Service are available at additional cost during the booking process.
6. If you need to change to a new departure date, only the paid amount will transfer over to the new booking.
7. Please redeem your voucher(s) and make tour reservation online. Please call us at 1-888-410-4111 during our working hours 9-6 PST, M-S
8. Your voucher is only valid for the specific gateway and date you selected on Groupon. When book, please Click ‘DATES & RATES’, then select your Departure City you purchased from Groupon, then select the Date, then fill in your information on the right side. Follow the steps by filling in your passport information (please provide your passport info within one week if you do not have it now, by sending e-mails to info@intertrips.com along with your booking number).
9. We will provide final documents (flight information, local contact number, hotel list etc.) in about one month before your departure date. If you have also purchased China Visa Service from us, our agent will contact you in about 2-3 months before your departure.
10. All the reservations are subject to InterTrips’ standard terms and conditions.
